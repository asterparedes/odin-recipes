Sinigang Recipe (Sour Soup)

This is the ultimate comfort food especially in rainy season in Philippines. You can make it with pork, beef, fish and shrimp.

Ingredients

  • 2 pound pork
  • 1 bunch bokchoy
  • 6 pieces string beans
  • 1 piece radish
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 pieces tomato quartered
  • 2 pieces chili
  • 2 quarts water
  • 3 pieces taro gabi
  • 1 pack sinigang mix
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saute onion and tomatoes.
  2. Add pork until light brown.
  3. Pour the water and bring to a boil.
  4. Add taro and radish then simmer for 40 minutes or until pork is tender.
  5. Add sinigang mix and chilli. Add the string beans and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add bokchoy. Serve hot.
