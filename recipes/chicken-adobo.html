Chicken Adobo Recipe

Traditional dish where every Filipino family loves to cook and eat. Eat with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saute onion and garlic.
  2. Add chicken until golden brown.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, water, and bay leaf.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to simmer.
  5. Simmer for 1 hour, or until chicken is very tender.
